About us

Qubed Q3 is a very diverse gaming community. We specialize in delivering quality fun and entertainment via livestreams. They're currently no requirements to join other then following the rules. You can join by doing practically anything. As part of our community you get to call yourself a "Qublet". The best place to chill and hangout with everyone is on the Qubed Q3 Discord which can be found @ http://discord.qubedq3.com/. If you like what you see, then come join us!
