Qubed Q3 Community Guidelines and Rules

1. General Guidelines for Qubed Q3

These guidelines and rules apply to everyone whether or not they are a member:
  1. Do not violate Discord or Twitch Terms of Service.

  2. Do not post anyones personal information regardless of what reason you may have.

  3. Do not advertise other groups that have no relation to Qubed Q3 or it's partners unless verbal or written permission is granted by an Administrator.

  4. Do not disrupt the commmunity in any shape or form.

  5. If you have a ton of background noise, please use Push-to-Talk. No one wants to hear it.

2. Rules

  1. Use common sense.

  2. Be respectful, mindful, and polite to everyone.

  3. All topics are to remain in the mindset of PG-13 or lower.

About us

Qubed Q3 is a very diverse gaming community. We specialize in delivering quality fun and entertainment via livestreams. They're currently no requirements to join other then following the rules. You can join by doing practically anything. As part of our community you get to call yourself a "Qublet". The best place to chill and hangout with everyone is on the Qubed Q3 Discord which can be found @ http://discord.qubedq3.com/. If you like what you see, then come join us!
